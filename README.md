### 1\. Eye Protection

  * **Recommendation:** Protective eyewear is highly encouraged for all players, especially during net play.
  * **Why:** To protect against stray balls, paddle collisions, and court debris.

### 2\. Footwear
  * **Warning:** Avoid running shoes with high heels/foam stacks as they increase the risk of ankle rolls.

### 3\. Paddle Maintenance
  * **Surface:** Ensure the paddle surface is not degraded (delaminated), which can deaden shots and cause arm strain.
